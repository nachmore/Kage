Help improve Kage
Share anonymous usage data so we know what to fix and build next
Kage is built for privacy-conscious users. If you'd rather not share anything, flip the toggle off — everything else still works. You can change this anytime in Settings → Privacy.
What we collect
  • A random install ID (not linked to you or your account)
  • App version, OS and version, language, country
  • High-level feature usage — which features are used and how often
What we never collect
  • Your messages, prompts, or AI responses
  • File names, paths, or clipboard contents
  • Your name, email, or IP address (stripped at ingest)
Read the full privacy policy for details.
